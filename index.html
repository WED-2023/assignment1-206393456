About Miriam Adelson

Miriam Adelson is an Israeli-American physician, philanthropist, and businesswoman. Born in 1945 in Tel Aviv, she became a specialist in internal medicine and addiction treatment. Alongside her late husband, Sheldon Adelson, she co-founded clinics to help individuals suffering from drug addiction in both Israel and the U.S. Miriam is also a major supporter of Jewish and Israeli causes, contributing to educational, medical, and cultural institutions. After Sheldon’s passing, she became the publisher of the newspaper Israel Hayom and one of the wealthiest women in the world. Her life combines science, philanthropy, and a deep commitment to Israel and the Jewish people.
